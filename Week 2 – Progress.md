**Attrition Analysis**
In Week 2, the focus was on analyzing employee attrition and understanding why employees are leaving the organization. What was done:
● Built key attrition-related DAX measures, including:
   ● Total Separations
   ● Voluntary Separations
   ● Involuntary Separations
   ● Attrition Rate (%)
 
 ● Designed an interactive Attrition Analysis dashboard in Power BI showing:
   ● Overall attrition rate and total separations
   ● Attrition rate trends by month
   ● Comparison of voluntary vs involuntary separations
   ● Key reasons for employee separations
● Added department and manager-level filters to allow deeper analysis and drill-down.
The goal of Week 2 was to identify attrition trends, understand separation drivers, and provide actionable insights to support employee retention strategies.
